RPE Max - Support

How to Use

Estimate 1RM: Enter the weight you lifted, reps completed, and the RPE of the set. Tap "Estimate 1RM" to calculate your estimated one-rep max.

Target Load: Enter your current 1RM, desired reps, and target RPE. Tap "Calculate Target Load" to find the weight you should use.

FAQ

What is RPE?
Rate of Perceived Exertion on a 1-10 scale. An RPE of 10 means no reps left in the tank. An RPE of 8 means you could have done 2 more reps.

Why is my result different from other calculators?
RPE Max uses a standard RPE percentage chart. Small differences between calculators are normal.
